# Spreadsheet Visualisation Activity

<details>

<summary>Step 1: Choose a Location</summary>

Select a location in Australia (e.g., Sydney, Melbourne, Cairns).

</details>

<details>

<summary>Step 2: Access Rainfall Data</summary>

* [Go to the BOM website.](https://www.bom.gov.au/climate/data/index.shtml?zoom=1\&lat=-26.9635\&lon=133.4635\&dp=IDC10002\&p_nccObsCode=139\&p_display_type=dataFile)

- Find the monthly or annual rainfall data for your chosen location:
  * Select **Monthly Rainfall**
  * **Enter a location of your preference** and click **Find**
  * Select a specific area (see below for Gosford for instance)
  * Click **Get Data**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (19).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* A new tab will open. Near the top right, click **All Years of Data**

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (21).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



</details>

<details>

<summary>Step 3: Organise the Data</summary>

* Filter the data down to the **last 7 years.**&#x20;

- Include columns for **Year/Month** and **Rainfall (mm)**.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Step 4: Analyse the Data</summary>

* Calculate average annual rainfall for the period using a spreadsheet formula.

- Identify the **wettest** and **driest** years or months.
  * **Extension:** Can you use a formula to do this automatically?

* Look for trends or patterns: is rainfall increasing, decreasing, or staying the same?
  * **Extension:** Can you use a formula to do this automatically?

</details>

<details>

<summary>Step 5: Visualise the Data</summary>

* Create a **line graph or bar chart** showing rainfall over time.

- Highlight any unusual spikes or drops.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Step 6: Reflection Questions</summary>

1. What trends did you notice in the rainfall data?
2. Were there any extreme years with much higher or lower rainfall than average?
3. How might this rainfall pattern affect farming, water supply, or daily life in your location?
4. If you compared this location to another city, how are the rainfall patterns similar or different?

</details>
